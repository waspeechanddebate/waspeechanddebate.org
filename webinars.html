Beyond the Ballot: A Conversation with Top Debaters

What: A conversation with top debaters about their journey

When: 7/24, 7 PM PST

Where: Online (Informaiton will be sent to email filled out in the form)

Sign-Up Now ⮕

Beyond the Ballot is a FREE Q&A webinar with college students that have previously debated. You can hear about their experience, the effect that debate has had on them, and more through our webinar!

Panel: Dessie Yang

Dessie is a rising sophomore at Princeton University studying Financial Engineering & Machine Learning. In high school, she competed in Public Forum debate for Bergen Tech in New Jersey for two years and served as co-captain of the debate team. During her debate career, she earned six bids to the Tournament of Champions and qualified for the TOC twice. Notably, she reached quarterfinals at Harvard and Season Opener, championed Seattle, and made it to octafinals at Harvard in 2024. She was also invited to compete in the Harvard and Florida Blue Key Round Robins.
